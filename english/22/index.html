English 22 - Change My Heart, O God

Verse
Change my heart, O God,
Make it ever true;
Change my heart, O God,
May I be like You.

Chorus
You are the Potter,
I am the clay;
Mould me and make me,
This is what I pray.

(Repeat Verse)

(Eddie Espinosa / Ref: Psalm 51:10)
