English 114 - All Heaven Declares

arrow_forward_ios

Verse 1
All heaven declares
The glory of the risen Lord
Who can compare
With the beauty of the Lord?
Forever He will be
The Lamb upon the throne
I gladly bow the knee
And worship Him alone.

Verse 2
I will proclaim
The glory of the risen Lord
Who once was slain
To reconcile man to God
Forever You will be
The Lamb upon the throne
I gladly bow the knee
And worship You alone.

(Noel and Tricia Richards / Ref: Psalm 19:1; Revelation 5:12)
