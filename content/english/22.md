**Verse**  
Change my heart, O God,  
Make it ever true;  
Change my heart, O God,  
May I be like You.

**Chorus**  
You are the Potter,  
I am the clay;  
Mould me and make me,  
This is what I pray.

**(Repeat Verse)**

_(Eddie Espinosa / Ref: Psalm 51:10)_
