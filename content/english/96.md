This is Your name, forever-  
The name by which,  
You are remembered by.  
No other name,  
In generations,  
This is Your name,  
This is Your name, forever-  
You are, the God of Abraham,  
You are, the God of Isaac,  
You are, the God of Jacob,  
You are, indeed my God.

_(Lim Kai Seong)_
