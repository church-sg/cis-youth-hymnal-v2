You are my hiding place,  
You always fill my heart with songs  
Of deliverance,  
Whenever I am afraid I will trust in You,  
I will trust in You,  
Let the weak say, “I am strong  
In the strength of the Lord.”  
**(Repeat, then sing last line)**

I will trust in you.”

_(Michael Ledner)_
